Miscellaneous Branding

Working at an agency, I have been able to work on a variety of projects that help keep my branding and traditional design skills sharp.

PTECH, Norwalk Early College Academy

IBM partners with Pathways in Technology Early College High School (PTECH) to create special STEM education programs. Norwalk Early College Academy was going to be the first school in Connecticut to follow this PTECH model and needed their own branding for the first graduates.

Events

IBM events were massive projects that we would work on every year. This is a tiny sampling of some of the concepts and touch points that were worked on for two of their biggest events. Deliverables would include exterior and interior building hero environmental graphics, signage, stage design, motion graphics, stationary, premiums, apps, web banners, etc.
